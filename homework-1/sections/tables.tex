Customers &

        \item ID: Unique number of the technical asssistant, Text

        \item Last name: Surname of the technical assistant, Text

    \end{itemize}

Warranty & 
 Represents terms of agreement between the customer and the seller after purchases made on the product 
 & \begin{itemize}
     \item ID: Identifier for the product warranty term, Serial

Invoice &
Represents listing and detailing the prices of all products that the customer has purchased from during a given period. & \begin{itemize}
    \item ID: A unique record number assigned to each invoice issued, Serial
    \item Date: Time-stamped date on which the transaction was recorded, Datetime 
    \end{itemize} & ID \\\hline
    
Device & Represents the product that are meant for sale. & \begin{itemize}
    \item ID: Unique number for a respective device, Serial
    \item Price: The price of the product, Int
    \item Name: The name of the product, Text
    \item Category: The set that the devices belong to, Text
\end{itemize} & ID \\\hline

 \end{itemize} & ID \\\hline
